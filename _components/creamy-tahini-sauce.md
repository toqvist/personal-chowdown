ingredients:
- garlic
- ginger
- lime juice
- toasted sesame oil
- maple syrup
- soy sauce
- tahini

directions:
- Grate garlic and ginger. Squeeze lime juice.
- Add toasted sesame oil, maple syrup, soy sauce and tahini
- Mix well!

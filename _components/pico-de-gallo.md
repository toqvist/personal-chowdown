ingredients:
- 1 red onion
- 1 serrano pepper
- 2 tbsp lime juice
- tomatoes
- half cup fresh cilantro

directions:
- Mince and mix red onion, serano pepper and lime juice
- Season with salt and mix well
- Chop and add tomatoes
- Chop and add cilantro
- Season with s&p

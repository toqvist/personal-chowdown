ingredients:
- 1 cup brown chickpeas
- 1 yellow onion
- 3 cloves garlic
- thumb ginger
- 1 can crushed tomatoes
- 1 bay leaf
- 0.5 tsp cumin seeds
- 1 tsp chana masala

directions:
- Soak chickpeas
- Brown cumin seeds and bay leaf
- Brown onions, garlic, ginger
- Add crushed tomatoes, chickpeas
- Pressure cook for 30 minutes
- Serve with rice and cilantro

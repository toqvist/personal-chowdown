ingredients:
- 1 yellow onion
- 2 cloves garlic
- 400g of black beans
- bbq sauce
- 1 tbsp smoked paprika
- 1 dl boiled rice
- 1 dl breadcrumbs

directions:
 - Soak beans overnight
 - Cook beans
 - Fry the onion and garlic
 - Mix together with beans into a container, add spices and blend into paste
 - Form burger patties with the paste.
 - Fry the patties
